Why it's better to party
on Saturdays than on Fridays

(Especially for women)

The following graphic shows the number of sexual crimes registered in Colombia during 2016, organized by sex of the victim and day of the week.
As you can see, most of the victims are women and the most dangerous day is Friday. Saturday, in contrast, has one of the lowest values.
